Able Framework

Ineqe's app framework was a creative challenge and I loved it. The framework is used for many different organisations and must be able to be used by children, professionals, parents and careres. We opted for a plyful interface that would keep the interest of all our target audience while they used the app to learn about staying safe in the digital world. I designed the UI for the app while considering UX. I also did all iconography and the illustration used within the app.

Travel App

I created high fidelity mock-ups for a travel app. My idea was a holiday randomizer app where you entered in the amount of people and your interests and it would book a surprise holiday for you! I designed the icons, characters and UI for this app.
